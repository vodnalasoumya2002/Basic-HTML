SHIZUKA MINAMOTO

Shizuka Minamoto, usually called Shizu-chan or Shizuka-chan, is the tritagonist of the Doraemon franchise. She is Nobita Nobi's childhood friend and crush who was born on May 8. She will eventually become Nobita's wife by the name of Shizuka Nobi, and the mother of his son, Nobisuke, in the future.
